EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Kenlight Mercy is a visionary entrepreneur, multi-certified CEO, mentor, and real estate developer dedicated to transforming businesses, communities, and leaders in Nigeria.

She is the President of Kenlight’s Group of Companies, a parent organization with subsidiaries across real estate, education, trade, fashion, consultancy, project management, food, and media. Her flagship venture, Kenlight’s Paradise Ltd, anchors her real estate empire and powers innovative platforms such as The Realtors Podcast with Kenlight and Kenlight’s School of Realty, both designed to educate and empower realtors, investors, and future business leaders.

Through her diverse ventures—including Kenlight’s Interiors & Designs, Kenlight’s Constructions, Kenlight’s Media, Kenlight’s Courtyard Ltd, Kenlight’s Academy, Kenlight’s Foods, Kenlight’s Consults, and Kenlight’s Couture—she has built a brand synonymous with excellence and innovation.

With over 8 years of experience, Kenlight Mercy has not only developed physical communities but also invested in the growth of people, achieving milestones that many twice her age aspire to. Born on 21st December in the late 1990s and hailing from Abiriba, Abia State, she continues to be celebrated as one of Nigeria’s outstanding entrepreneurial figures.
